The 10-second countdown before "Continua" unlocks on the review prompt is intentional. The goal is to maximize the chance that satisfied customers leave a Google/platform review before getting their reward. Short countdown = users skip straight to reward.

**Why:** The core business value of 5stelle is driving positive reviews to public platforms. The countdown ensures users engage with the review CTA.
**How to apply:** Never suggest reducing or removing the countdown. Treat it as a deliberate business decision.
